2.2 HOCI® (Continuous Improvement Management System)
HOCI® provides a preventive-based continuous improvement management system for all employees to continuously improve their processes. All employees can utilize SDMS-PC® (SDMS Performance Cards) to identify, monitor, and eliminate the root causes of all problems. Furthermore, corporate management is able to identify, monitor and manage the key-of-the-keys (KOFK®) processes, and prevent any likely disastrous problems from happening in the bud.

HIMS® and HOCI® ensure the root causes of all problems are identified, monitored, and eliminated in the bud
HIMS®, HOCI®, and SDMS-PC® identify and monitor all problems which are aligned to the corporate goals and eliminate their root causes in the bud
HIMS®, HOCI® and KOFK® ensure that the management can identify and monitor the key-of- the-keys disastrous problems and nip them in the bud. Thereafter, HIMS® will automatically select the next batch of the key-of-the-keys problems for elimination.
