3.1.1 HIMS-QMSTM (HIMS Quality Management System)

Benefits

Synergic Benefits: Improve Quality, Reduce Cost, Enhance Productivity

Functions

When HIMS® is integrated with ISO Quality Management System, HIMS® will ensure that the organization:
1. aligns operational goals of all employees to corporate goals,
2. complies with the ISO9001 quality requirements, 3. implement strategic pans effectively.

Outcomes

1. Improve organizational quality.

2. Dispense with most of the paperwork and internal audit work.

3. Achieve organizational goals and move towards business excellence
