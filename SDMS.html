2.1 SDMS® (Strategic Deployment Management System)

SDMS® is the key pillar of HIMS®. SDMS® translates the mission of the organization into measurable corporate goals, which are then cascaded into strategic goals, which are finally cascaded into operational goals. Similarly, the vision of the organization is translated into measurable strategic thrusts, which are then cascaded into strategic goals, which are finally cascaded into operational initiatives.

BENEFITS
Productivity Gain
Integration Functions
HIMS® and SDMS® align all employee goals to corporate goals, mission, and vision
HIMS® and SDMS® ensure all employees focus on doing value added works, and removing all wastes from all processes
Outcomes
The organization productivity will increase immediately by at least 20%-50%
