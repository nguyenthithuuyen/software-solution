3.1.2 HIMS-IRSTM (HIMS Incentive and Rewarding System)
Benefits
Synergic Benefits: Improve Quality, Reduce Cost, Enhance Productivity
Functions
When HIMS® is integrated with IRS Incentive and Rewarding System, HIMS® will ensure that the organization:
1. aligns operational goals of all employees to corporate goals, 2. motivate and reward all employees in a fair, just and open management system, 3. implement strategic pans effectively.
Outcomes
1. Achieve aligned operational goals.

2. Achieve organizational core values.

3. Achieve organizational goals and move towards business excellence.

4. It is a win-win situation. Both the organization and employees benefit from HIMS-IRSTM
